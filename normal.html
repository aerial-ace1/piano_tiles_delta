How to Play?
  1. Press Play
  2. Remember the tiles that lit up
  3. Click on all the those tile and go to the next round
  4. Number of tiles increases by 1 for each round
  5. Hacker Mode requires you to press keys in the same order they lit up but gives double points
  6. Play and make your way onto the LeaderBoard
